Michael Phelps

Michael Phelps is the most decorated Olympian of all time. He has won a total of 28 Olympic medals, including 23 gold medals. Phelps is known for his incredible butterfly and freestyle swimming skills.

Caeleb Dressel

Caeleb Dressel is a sprint specialist known for his explosive speed in the pool. He has won multiple Olympic gold medals and holds several American records. Dressel excels in freestyle and butterfly events.
